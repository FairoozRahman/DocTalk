# La LaLa La La
DocTalk is Bangladesh's first complete web application that will allow patients and doctors to communicate online, get E-prescription, read and write blogs, buy medicines and ask and answer their general queries. People will choose to stay at home and conduct their meetings online due to the COVID scenario. It will be the ideal platform for both doctors and patients to conduct online consultations. Our app will make consultations secure and simple, reducing time while also protecting from Covid. It will facilitate communication between patients and doctors. There will be an interactive portal where doctors will respond to various inquiries from patients, saving them time and providing them with reliable information. People will be able to find authentic pharmaceuticals on the e-commerce website.
